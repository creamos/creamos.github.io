About Me

Hello there!
I'm Alexandre Lacroix, a junior gameplay programmer soon to be a proud graduate with a Master's degree from a well-regarded national university in France.

I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, such as developing an ecosystem simulation, working on a boardgame-inspired strategy game and even a fast-paced parkour-oriented 3rd-person game.
I'm very curious and I don't hesitate to explore new areas in the programming sphere. From the basics of engine programming and shaders with the prototyping of a Minecraft clone in pure C++, to the experimentation of data-oriented architecture using the Unity DOTS framework.
While I mostly work on game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, my curiosity led me to explore more exotic ones like Pico-8.
